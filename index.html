About the Route Setters

Who we are

Harpeth Valley Route Setters is a Middle Tennessee nonprofit dedicated to two things at once: keeping our outdoor spaces clean and healthy, and giving young people a real reason to fall in love with them.

We organize cleanups at climbing crags, hiking trails, and the natural areas around them, restoring what's been worn down or left behind. Alongside that work, we run climbing competitions, hiking excursions, and other outdoor events that raise funds for our conservation efforts and bring teens and young adults into the outdoor community.

Our goal is simple: a Tennessee outdoors that's cleaner, more accessible, and more welcoming — to people of every background and identity — for the long run.

  • Clean and restore climbing areas, trails, and surrounding environments
  • Fund the work through friendly competitions and outdoor excursions
  • Open the door to climbing and hiking for teens and young adults across Middle Tennessee
  • Build a community rooted in shared care for the outdoors

Harpeth Valley Route Setters has applied for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status with the IRS. Our application is currently pending.
